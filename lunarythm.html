how it works

Designed to feel like relief, not another compliance module.

Each member gets a plan that acknowledges real life: kids, roommates, pets, commuting, grocery windows, alert fatigue, and the hard emotional gear-shift after a critical night.

Import the rotation

Connect a work calendar, screenshot a schedule, or enter a repeating pattern. Lunarythm labels the recovery pinch points.

Set the household rules

Add quiet hours, family notifications, commute buffers, caffeine cutoffs, meals, and “please don’t ask me before noon” windows.

Activate the night kit

Turn on low-light reminders, smart-home cues, delivery holds, handoff notes, and optional concierge errands.

Review the rhythm

Every two weeks, the plan adapts around missed sleep windows, stressful handoffs, and upcoming schedule flips.

faq

Questions a real buyer might ask.

Is Lunarythm medical advice?

No. In this fictional concept, Lunarythm offers planning, reminders, household coordination, and concierge support. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace clinician guidance.

Does my employer see my sleep details?

No. Team plans receive aggregate patterns such as adoption, common friction windows, and schedule pressure. Individual routines stay private unless the member shares them.

Why this palette?

The dark Mocha colors make the product feel native to night work, while Latte sections make product explanations feel clear and daylight-friendly.

Does the website work on iPhone?

Yes. The layout collapses to one column, the navigation becomes a hamburger menu, tap targets remain large, and the CTA appears only after scrolling past the hero.
